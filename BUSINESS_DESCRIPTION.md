# CloseBuy - Business Description Document

## Executive Summary

**CloseBuy** CloseBuy is a hyperlocal e-commerce mobile application that connects estate residents with nearby vendors, enabling convenient same-day delivery within the estate. Users can browse products, place orders, and receive deliveries without leaving their neighborhood.

## Business Description

### What We Do

CloseBuy is a mobile marketplace application that enables residents to:
- **Shop locally**: Browse products from nearby vendors and stores
- **Order conveniently**: Place orders directly through the mobile app
- **Get fast delivery**: Receive purchases within the same day
- **Pay securely**: Multiple payment options including card and cash on delivery
- **Track orders**: Monitor delivery status in real-time

### Our Mission

To provide a seamless shopping experience for local communities by connecting residents with nearby vendors, promoting local businesses, and ensuring convenient access to daily essentials without leaving the neighborhood.

### Our Vision

To become the leading hyperlocal e-commerce platform in Nigeria, expanding to serve multiple residential estates and communities with efficient, reliable, and affordable delivery services.
